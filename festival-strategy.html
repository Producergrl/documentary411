Your Premiere Is Not the Finish Line. It Is the Starting Gun.

The complete 90-day preparation playbook for documentary and independent filmmakers, from choosing the right festivals to the distribution conversations that follow.

Most filmmakers start preparing for a festival about three weeks out. They book flights, send a few emails, show up excited, and come home exhausted, with a handful of business cards and very little traction. Done well, a festival premiere is a media moment, a distribution trigger, and a networking accelerator. Done poorly, it is an expensive weekend away.

The difference between those two outcomes is not talent. It is preparation. This guide is the tried and tested strategy, phase by phase, week by week, from a producing team that has stood on festival stages accepting awards 75+ times, domestically and internationally.
