# Grant and Fund Researcher — Copy & Paste Edition

No installation. No files to upload. No API keys. Nothing technical.

## The three steps

1. **Connect Gmail to Claude** (one time only): go to claude.ai →
   Settings → Connectors → find Gmail → click Connect and approve with
   your Google login. Claude can only create drafts for you to review —
   it can never send an email.

2. **Open the file `MASTER-PROMPT.txt`** that came with this purchase.
   Select everything in it (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A), and copy it.

3. **Open the Claude desktop app, switch to Cowork mode, start a new
   session, and paste the whole thing in.** (Cowork matters: it's the
   mode where Claude can create your spreadsheet and schedule the
   nightly run. Regular chat can't do either.)
   Claude takes over from there: it interviews you about your project,
   builds your funder-tracking spreadsheet, drafts a sample letter into
   your Gmail for your approval, and schedules the nightly research run.

That's the entire setup. About five minutes of answering questions.

## What you need

- A paid Claude plan with the **Claude desktop app** (the setup runs in
  Cowork mode; the researcher runs as scheduled tasks on your own
  account — there are no extra fees, subscriptions, or API keys)
- Gmail connected, per step 1

## Every night after that

Claude researches funders and sponsors matched to your project's
mission, verifies every organization and email address against a real
web page, logs up to 5 new leads in your spreadsheet, and drafts
personalized letters of inquiry into your Gmail under your project's
label. You wake up, review, edit, and send the ones you like.
**Nothing is ever sent without you.**

Pause it, change the run time, or shut it down any time by simply
asking Claude in any conversation.
